          TAILORED TO PERFORM
            

                                                Every technical consideration has been translated into an aesthetic design. The most
                                                provocative proportions of a modern BUGATTI ever, and the quintessence of our Design
                                                Ethos: "Forme follows performance."


                                                                                            

Extremely lightweight. High strength.

Liberating the car of all baggage, the newly-shaped, lightweight carbon monocoque — which had to be constructed around the incredibly compact drivetrain — bestows full aerodynamic effectiveness all over.
Phenomenal Downforce

An ultra-light vehicle mastering extreme cornering speeds, yet manageable for all drivers, demands harnessing nearly three tons of downforce — a powerful testament to the groundbreaking aerodynamics, uncompromising design, and low center of gravity.
Mastering the air

A more prominent front diffuser, along with air curtains and a carefully designed underfloor, create a suction effect while minimizing drag as much as possible.



          BESPOKE EMOTIONS
            



                                                    Depending on the chassis setting, drivers can adapt the airflow and aero-balance of
                                                    their vehicles — granting owners access to unparalleled motorsport performance
                                                    levels through the BOLIDE’s highly focused aero package


                                                                                                 


BUGATTI ESSENCE

The horseshoe conveys the BUGATTI DNA, while providing structural and aerodynamic functions.
LASTING IMPRESSION

Invoking the X-planes of aviation history, the 'X' signature is used throughout the design. It is visible from every perspective and highlighted by X-shaped taillights that frame the striking new central exhaust configuration.
SPORT PROTOTYPE AMBIANCE

Reduced and minimal, the BOLIDE's cockpit draws inspiration from the LMP1 race cars.



          MOTORSPORT DYNAMICS

            



                                                    To achieve the exceptional driving performance and handling dynamics of an LMP1 race
                                                    car, a state-of-the-art push-rod suspension architecture was developed.                                               


                                                                        


                 BRAKES: PERFORMANCE AND INNOVATION

                 Similar to Formula 1, the BOLIDE utilizes carbon disks and pads for deceleration,
                 paired with lightweight monobloc calipers aided by dedicated cooling ducts.
